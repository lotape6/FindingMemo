What is this?

Finding Memo is an open-source learning platform where each lesson teaches AI concepts by being a working example of them. The slides, theme, and this page were all created by an AI agent -- demonstrating the workflow you're here to learn.

The core metaphor maps Finding Nemo to AI: Marlin is the agent, the Water Tunnels are the harness, and you (the human) build the tunnels.
